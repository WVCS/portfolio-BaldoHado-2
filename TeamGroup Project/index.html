About Us

UDG was developed in 2017 by Adam Ta. Adam Ta was a gaming addict that loved watching esports and also was fairly wealthy. He made the decision to create the eSports team FKC, which didn't work out well and was a failure. After revising his plan and fixing his mistakes, he founded UDG. The first game UDG competed in was League of Legends. Fast forward a few years and now we compete in 10+ games, ranging from games like Hearthstone to FPS (first person shooter) games like Valorant.
